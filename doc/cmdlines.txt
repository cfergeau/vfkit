## Start vfkit with gvproxy

term1> rm /Users/teuf/dev/vfkit/gvproxy.sock ; ./bin/gvproxy  --debug --listen unix:///tmp/gvproxy-http.sock --listen-vfkit unixgram:///Users/teuf/dev/vfkit/gvproxy.sock
term2> rm /tmp/vfkit.sock ; sh ./contrib/scripts/start-crc-bundle.sh ~/.crc/cache/crc_podman_vfkit_4.4.1_arm64

(vfkit is started with `--device virtio-net,unixSocketPath=gvproxy.sock,mac=5a:94:ef:e4:0c:ee`)
